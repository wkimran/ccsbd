Wherever you are in Bangladesh, in whatever industry, you can rely on our competent teams of experts to provide you value added assessment and evaluation to give your business entity an international recognition. We partner with clients to offer independent auditing services that will help you reduce risk, streamline processes and operate in a more sustainable manner.

Dr. Sied Sadek, CCSBD

CCSBD – Our founders have more than 20 years of Auditing & Implementation experience.

CCS is Third Party independent audit firm registered in Bangladesh under The Companies Act 1994, with international accreditation and recognition. Its main operation subject is assessment, verification and certification of Management systems (Quality, Environment, Occupational health and safety, Information security, Food safety, Energy), Social Compliance standards (SA8000, WRAP, BSCI, SEDEX), Organic standard (GOTS, OCS), Brand COC and Training courses on ISO another international Standards.

CCS is strategically positioned to provide independent audit services utilizing local auditors who are familiar with local cultures related to industries, social conditions, cultures and languages, industry practices, and the applicable laws. CCS evaluates supplier compliance with a variety of standards, including corporate codes of conduct containing standard industry compliance elements, and a range of industry Standards established by recognized international organizations.
